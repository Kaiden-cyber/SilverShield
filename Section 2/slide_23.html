Securing a Breached Account

Steps to secure your account if it has been hacked:

1. Don't panic, if your data has appeared in a breach this does not mean they have logged into your account

2. Decide if you need this account anymore, if not delete it. Otherwise, change the password to something much more secure

3. Add this new password into your password manager

4. If you have reused this password on any other accounts change them immediately.
