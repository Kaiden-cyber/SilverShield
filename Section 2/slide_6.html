Keeping your Password Secure

Why Change my Password?
I wish it was as simple as setting your password up and forgetting about it. Companies are constantly being hacked and the passwords of their users get stolen in the process.
How to Prevent it
The easiest way to prevent this is to update your password every 6 months to a year. Another great way to keep your password secure is to enter your email into haveibeenpwned.com and check if it has appeared in a breach.
