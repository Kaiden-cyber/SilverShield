Mission Statement

Creating a safer online environment by empowering senior citizens to navigate the digital world safely through education, resources, and support.

Goals

  • Raise Cybersecurity awareness
  • Provide Resources to Educate Senior Citizens
    • Provide simple and easy to access online interactive guides focused on avoiding online scams as well as protecting accounts and personal information
  • Collaborate with other Organizations to expand impact
  • Continuous Improvement
    • Continue to grow the resources available to senior citizens
    • Continue to improve upon the Phish Detective Chrome Extension
