Job Offer Scam

Click Email Samples in the Guide for sample scams
What is it?
This scam will come from someone messaging you on a hiring site telling you they have a job offer and will pay you a lot for a little work.
How to Prevent it
This scam is especially tricky to spot. Remember that you should not have to pay for a job, if it is too good to be true, it is, and do research before sending any informaiton.
