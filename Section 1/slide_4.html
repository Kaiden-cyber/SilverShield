Invoice Scam

Click Email Samples in the Guide for sample scams
What is it?
In this scam you receive an email saying that you have been charged a large amount for something you did not purchase. The scam email then says to click a link to cancel the charges
How to Prevent it
Double check the senders email, and don't click any links. If you suspect the charges are real go to the website directly instead of clicking the link
