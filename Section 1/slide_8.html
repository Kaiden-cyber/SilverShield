Document Sharing Scam

Click Email Samples in the Guide for sample scams
What is it?
You will get an email from Google Docs or another document sharing platform that says a document was shared and you need to click a link and login to view it.
How to Prevent it
This scam is easily spotted, if you weren't expecting the document delete it, double check the sender email, and don't click any links.
