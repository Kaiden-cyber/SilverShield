IRS/Tax Refund Scam

Click Email Samples in the Guide for sample scams
What is it?
This scam will claim to come from the IRS and ask for personal information for a tax refund, or they will scare you into sending money.
How to Prevent it
The IRS does not email you, they send paper mail to your house. Additionally, check the email, if it doesnt say @irs.gov delete it. More information: here
