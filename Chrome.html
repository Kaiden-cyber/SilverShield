Chrome Extension

What Does it do?

  • Provides you with a guide to catch phishing emails
  • Allows you to easily view the real email of a sender
  • Allows you to view the url that links are taking you to
  • Creates a checkpoint to verify suspicious or accidentially clicked on links
  • Includes an easy way to run the link through an antivirus

How Do I install it?

  1. Click here
  2. Press add to Chrome!

Once installed you need to click on the extension for it to load in your options
