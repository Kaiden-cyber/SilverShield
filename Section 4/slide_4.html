HaveIBeenPwned

HaveIBeenPwned is an easy and free way to check if an account has been compromised

Go to: https://haveibeenpwned.com/ and then enter your email. It will then show if your password has appeared in data breaches..

Dont worry if you have a compromised account! Revisit the Password and Account Security section to know what to do next.
