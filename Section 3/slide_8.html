Family Emergency Scams

This is currently the most common and hardest to spot scam. In this scam you will receive a call supposedly from a family memeber in jail, sometimes in a foreign jail, saying that they need money.

The most foolproof way to prevent these scams is to ask personal questions such as: What color is your car? What is the name of your childhood pet? What is your favorite food? Be warned that scammers commonly use AI voice cloners to sound closer to someone you know, and they can clone cell phone numbers to appear trustworthy. If you are still unsure if the call is real, look up the phone number of the jail they are supposedly being held in and call and ask if the person is actually in custody.
